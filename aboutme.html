The Wolf-Man

Powers & Mission:
Wolf man resists foes with a lethal club made from silver. Additionally, he wages war using a dangerous array of fangs and he possesses the power to make men appear from nowhere, however only during a new moon. wolf man lords over a pack of rats and he rides a super truck into battle. He likewise can rocket between dimensions and he can tunnel through solid corbomite, but never while carrying his medallion. wolf man has the ability to become incorporeal, but doing so makes him unlucky for one minute. Unfortunately, wolf man is well-known for being confused.
